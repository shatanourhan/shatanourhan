Conclusions

This research demonstrates that rotation fundamentally changes quantum black hole thermodynamics. The interplay between frame-dragging, quantum corrections, and angular momentum transfer creates physics completely absent in spherically symmetric cases.

Our systematic parameter space exploration reveals that while GSL remains technically satisfied, the marginal behavior for co-rotating particles in the quantum regime suggests we may be missing important quantum information-theoretic contributions to the entropy balance.
